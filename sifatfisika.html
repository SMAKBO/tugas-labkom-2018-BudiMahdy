Physical Properties



Physical Properties of Diethyl Ether are :

  • Ether is a colorless liquid

  • Ether is a volatile liquid

  • Ether also has a distinctive odor

  • Ether is not soluble in water

  • Ether is soluble in nonpolar solvent

  • Ether flammable with clear flame because ether vapor forms an explosive mixture with air

  • Ether can dissolve fats, oils, resins, alkaloids, bromine, and iods

  • The short chain C ether compound is a liquid at room temperature and its Boiling Point rises with the addition of Carbon Atom ( C )

  • The same element C TD ether > TD alkane and < TD alcohol
