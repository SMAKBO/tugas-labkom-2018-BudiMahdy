Chemical Properties




Chemical properties of diethyl ether, are:

  • Ether is slightly polar because the corner of the C - O - C bond is 110 degrees, so that C - O dipole can not negate each other.

  • Ether is more polar than alkanes, but not as polar as alcohol, esther, or amide. Eventough, the existence of two free electron pairs on oxygen atoms of ether allows the ether to have hydrogen bonding with water molecules.

  • Ether can be completely seperated by destillation.

  • In general ether has a low chemical reactivity, although it is more reactive than alkanes.

  • Flammable.

  • Generally toxic.

  • Anaesthetic (anesthetized).

  • Ether is difficult to react, except with strong halide acid (HI and HBr)
